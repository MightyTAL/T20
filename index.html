About

Hi, I am Aoling Tang, a PhD student in Management at Frankfurt School of Finance & Management. I completed my BSc and MSc in Economics at Beihang University.

My research interests lie in organizational decision making, motivated by the broad question of how people make decisions and why those decisions matter.

I am currently interested in screening and learning problems, especially how organizations select, evaluate, and update under uncertainty.

I value conversations that help refine research questions and perspectives. Please feel free to contact me!

Current Focus

I am thinking about the relationship between screening and learning in organizational decision making: how organizations filter alternatives, interpret feedback, and revise beliefs over time.
