About

Hi — I'm Aoling Tang. I am currently a PhD student at Frankfurt School of Finance & Management. My research centers on data-driven decision-making under uncertainty, with an emphasis on organizational applications and empirical methods.

Keywords: data-driven decision-making · uncertainty · empirical analysis · organizational studies

Research

Current projects explore how firms use data, analytics, and algorithms to make decisions in uncertain environments, and how those processes affect organizational outcomes. Methodologically I work with firm-level panel data, causal inference techniques, and quantitative modeling.
