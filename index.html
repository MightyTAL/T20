About

Hi, I am Aoling Tang, a PhD student in management at Frankfurt School of Finance & Management. I completed my BSc and MSc in Economics at Beihang University.

My research interests lie in organizational decision making, motivated by the broad research question of "how do people make decisions and why does it matter?" I'm very interested in screening and learning problems!

I value conversations that help refine research questions and perspectives. Please feel free to contact me!
