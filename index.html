About

Hi — I'm Aoling Tang. I am currently a PhD student focusing on how digital technologies affect organizational innovation and sustainability. My work bridges empirical analysis and policy-relevant research.

Keywords: data-driven innovation · green innovation · sustainability · corporate governance · organization theory

Research

Current projects examine the role of data-driven innovation in firms' environmental performance, the mediating role of green innovation, and how corporate environmental responsibility moderates these relationships. Methodologically I work with firm-level panel data and causal inference techniques.
